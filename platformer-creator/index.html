Platformer creator

Controls

  • move with arrow keys or WASD keys
  • left click to build block
  • press space to switch modes
  • scroll the mouse to change blocks
  • press Esc to pause game
  • press R to rotate blocks
  • press E to switch to the block you are pointing
  • press L to change long of fire bars or rotating saws

About game

  • In this game you can easy and fast make your own platformer!
  • Share your platformer using code and play other players platformers.
  • current version: beta 1.1.1
