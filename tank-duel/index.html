Tank Duel

Controls of blue

  • press arrow keys to move
  • press M or 0 to shot
  • press N or 1 to launch a missile, press againg to deploy it

Controls of red

  • press WASD to move
  • press SPACE to shot
  • press C to to launch a missile, press againg to deploy it

Controls

  • press ESC to pause the game

about game

  • the tank has the task of destroying the other tank
  • missiles can go through walls unlike tanks and bullets
  • lives are displayed above the tank, if not, the tank has full lives
  • bullets and missiles can destroy wooden boxes
  • oil slows down the tank
